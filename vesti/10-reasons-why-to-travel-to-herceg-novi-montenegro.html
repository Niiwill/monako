10 reasons why you should choose Igalo - Herceg Novi - for your next vacation

Looking for the perfect place for your next vacation? Think about Igalo! This city in Montenegro has a lot to offer, including beautiful beaches, rich history, cultural events, sports and recreation, spa and wellness, delicious food and drink, good weather, good prices, beautiful nature and proximity to other tourist destinations. This article presents 10 reasons why you should choose Igalo for your next vacation. Read more and find out why Igalo is a fantastic choice for your vacation!


1 Beaches for every taste

The beaches in Igalo are one of the most beautiful on the Montenegrin coast. Whether you are looking for a beach with sand or rocks, with lots of fun or peace and quiet, you will find something for you in Igalo. The warm and crystal clear waters of the Adriatic Sea, combined with small bays, give a magical experience of being on the beaches of Igalo.

2 Climate

Igalo is a town located in Montenegro, on the coast of the Adriatic Sea. The climate in Igalo is Mediterranean, which means that summers are hot and dry, and winters are mild and wet. The average annual temperature in Igalo is around 16°C. Summers in Igalo are warm and sunny, with an average air temperature of around 25-30°C. The hottest months are July and August. During the summer, there is little rain in Igalo, and the sea is warm and pleasant for swimming. Nights are also pleasant and warm, with temperatures rarely dropping below 20°C. Winters in Igalo are mild and wet, with an average temperature of around 10-12°C. The coldest months are January and February. Rain is frequent and snow is rare. The sea cools down during the winter, but the temperature rarely drops below 15°C. In spring and autumn, temperatures are moderate, with average temperatures of around 15-20°C. These periods are ideal for visiting Igalo if you don't like too much heat and want to avoid the crowds of tourists. Along with plenty of sunshine, Igalo also has a high level of humidity, especially during winter. Also, it is important to note that Igalo is known for strong winds, which can affect the pleasantness of staying in this city. Therefore, it is always good to check the weather forecast before visiting Igalo.

3 Rich history

Igalo has a long and rich history, with many interesting stories and legends. In the past, this city was ruled by the Romans, Turks and Austria-Hungary, and traces of these cultures can still be seen throughout the city.


4 Health and wellness

Igalo is known as one of the best places for treating rheumatic diseases and injuries. This city has a natural healing factor, which makes it an ideal place for recovery. In addition, in Igalo you can find numerous wellness centers and spa centers where you can enjoy healing mud treatments and other wellness treatments.


5 Gastronomy

Montenegrin cuisine is rich and varied, and in Igalo you will have the opportunity to try many specialties from this cuisine. From fresh fish and seafood to local cheeses and meat products, the food in Igalo will satisfy everyone's taste.

6 Outdoor activities

If you are looking for adventure, Igalo has a lot to offer. You can enjoy various outdoor activities such as kayaking, diving, hiking and cycling.

7 Nightlife

The largest concentration of night clubs, bars and discotheques is located near the Marina in the city center, where there is a lively atmosphere throughout the year. There you can find a large number of different bars that offer different music genres, from pop and rock to electronic music and house. One of the most popular nightclubs in Herceg Novi is "Club CASA", which is located in the heart of Igalo. This club offers a variety of music genres, including RnB, house and techno and folk. For those looking for something less crowded, you can visit the numerous bars and clubs located along the beach. It is important to note that nightlife in Herceg Novi is mostly active during the tourist season, which lasts from May to September. During the other months, the nightlife is less lively, but you can still find some bars and clubs that are open all year round.

8 Affordable prices

When it comes to vacations, prices can be quite high, but in Igalo this is not the case. This city offers a wide variety of accommodation for every budget, so you're sure to find something to suit your needs. Also, food and drink are relatively cheap, so you can enjoy delicious food and drink without feeling a huge strain on your wallet.

9 Beautiful nature

Igalo is surrounded by beautiful nature, and the most beautiful scenes are located along the coast. The scenery in this region is beautiful, with crystal clear sea and green hills in the background. It is an ideal place for taking photos or just enjoying nature.

10 Proximity to other tourist destinations

Igalo is the perfect place to stay if you want to explore other parts of Montenegro, such as Herceg Novi, Kotor or Budva. All these cities are easily accessible, and the journey between them takes only a few hours.

As you can see, Igalo is a fantastic holiday destination, with plenty of things to give you an exciting experience. From beautiful beaches and delicious food to history and nature, Igalo has a lot to offer. If you are looking for the perfect place to relax, have fun and explore, Igalo is definitely the place to visit!
